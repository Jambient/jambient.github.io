What does it take to decompress PNGs?

11th December 2024

Nowadays, there are so many image loading packages that there is no need to understand how they work, you can just insert one of them into a project and instantly have working image loading. However have you ever wondered how compressed formats such as PNG work?

While working on my OpenGL graphics project, I thought it would be an interesting challenge to get PNG decompression working. I managed to accomplish this and it was interesting enough to write an article about.
